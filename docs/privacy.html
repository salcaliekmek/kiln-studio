Kilnly

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective date: April 2026

Kilnly does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — materials, production records, kiln firings, stock — is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

1. Who We Are

Kilnly is a ceramic studio production management application developed for independent potters and ceramic studios. The app is operated by Ercüment Kıkıllı (ercmntkll@gmail.com).

2. Data We Store

Kilnly stores the following data locally on your device using SQLite (via expo-sqlite):

  • Raw materials and purchase records
  • Product catalog and recipes
  • Production batches and stage tracking
  • Kiln firing schedules and history
  • Stock levels and inventory
  • Color recipes and slip batches
  • Studio settings (name, owner name)
  • Electricity price history

This data is stored only on your device. It is never uploaded to any server, cloud, or third-party service.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account registration or login required
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No location or GPS data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting
  • No third-party tracking SDKs (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude)
  • No advertising networks

4. Third-Party Services

Kilnly does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The only external dependency is Expo (the development framework), which is used solely to build and distribute the application and does not collect user data through the app at runtime.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your data with anyone — because we never have access to it in the first place. Your data stays on your device, under your full control.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time directly within the app (Profile → Reset Data) or by uninstalling Kilnly from your device. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

Kilnly is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
